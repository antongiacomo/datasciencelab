### Abstract

Drawing on three years of R&D for a major Polish financial institution, this talk shares insights from the development and deployment of customer data deduplication pipelines. I will outline the journey of building two distinct end-to-end deduplication pipelines: one rooted in statistical modeling (currently live in production) and another - leveraging machine learning. Beyond the case study, I will outline the evolution of deduplication techniques, including standard approaches as well as modern machine learning and neural network solutions.
